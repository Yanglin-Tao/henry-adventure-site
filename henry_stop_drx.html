Henry soon found Dr. X. When Dr. X explained the situation to Henry, Henry stood dumbfounded, not realizing that the cause of everything was actually the small glowing object in the grass decades ago. With Henry's persuasion, Dr. X was enlightened and immediately aborted the replication program before it was too late. In fact, Dr. X expected the possible outcome at the very beginning, so he only sent the replica to Henry to test the reaction.
