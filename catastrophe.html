With the help of Dr. X, more and more people are gaining access to infinite knowledge through glasses. But from the beginning to the end, Dr. X did not realize that the absolute fairness he identified in his heart was not suitable for human society.

Humans started using knowledge for everything, which made people not afraid of anything, and the power of emptiness released the demons in people's hearts. People use knowledge to destroy each other's lives, have knowledge to make decisions beyond ethics, killing, politics, wars, human rights, and civilization.

The human race finally came to its end, and the little pairs of glasses destroyed the civilization that had lasted until today...
